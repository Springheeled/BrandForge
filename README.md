# BrandForge

Internal brand discovery engine for Bellamy Technologies Ltd.

## Purpose

BrandForge is an internal engineering tool used to discover, evaluate and shortlist long-term company and product brands.

It exists to support Bellamy Technologies Ltd and its products. It is **not** intended to become a commercial product unless a future strategic decision is made.

## Current Mission

Discover the long-term public brand for the commerce operations platform currently known as PicPac.

## Design Principles

- Meaning over randomness.
- Calm over hype.
- Human over corporate.
- British.
- Professional.
- Memorable.
- Built for long-term brands.
- "Grows with you."

## Current Architecture

BrandForge currently contains:

- Candidate generation
- Pronunciation filtering
- Scoring
- CSV export

## Immediate Roadmap

### Sprint 1
Replace random prefix/middle/suffix generation with meaning-led generation driven by brand DNA and emotional territories.

### Sprint 2
Improve rhythm, pronunciation and memorability scoring.

### Sprint 3
Introduce domain-first candidate evaluation.

## Repository Rules

- Small green slices.
- Compile → Test → Review → Commit.
- Repository is the source of truth.
- Avoid speculative architecture.
- Every sprint must improve the quality of generated brands.
